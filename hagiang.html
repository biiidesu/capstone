Ha Giang province borders China in northern Vietnam. This remote, stunning location is perfect for travelers seeking a multi-day trekking experience. Through the beautiful mountainous rural of Ha Giang, Tam Son, Yen Minh, Dong Van and Meo Vac town, you will discover Red Dao culture, Hmong culture, Tay culture , Local Homestay, Hmong King Palace, Lung Cu flag tower on top of Dragon Mountain in Lung Cu village the northernmost point of Vietnam, Ma Pi Leng pass, local markets Explore the highlights of Ha Giang Province with local guides. You will experience beautiful landscapes, cultures, historical monument and famous landmarks. Itinerary
